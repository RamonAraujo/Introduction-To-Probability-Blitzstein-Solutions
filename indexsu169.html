4.3.12 problem 52

Let Ij be the indicator variable for the j-toss landing on an outcome different from the previous toss for 2≤j≤n. Then, the total number of such tosses is X=∑nj=2Ij. The total number of runs is Y=X+1. Since E(X)=∑nj=2P(Ij=1)=∑nj=212=n−12, E(Y)=n−12+1=n+12.
