4.3.3 problem 40

Let Ij be the indicator random variable for HTH pattern starting on the j-th toss. Since the tosses are independent, P(Ij=1)=18 for 1≤j≤n−2.

Let X=∑n−2j=1Ij be the number of HTH patterns in n independent coin tosses. Then,

E(X)=E(n−2∑j=1Ij)=n−2∑j=1E(Ij)=n−2∑j=118=n−28
