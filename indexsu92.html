2.6.9 problem 68

(a)
OR=P(D|C)P(Dc|C)∗P(Dc|Cc)P(D|Cc)

Since the disease is rare among both exposed and not exposed groups, P(Dc|C)≈1 and P(Dc|Cc)≈1. Thus,

OR≈P(D|C)P(D|Cc)=RR

(b)
P(C,D)P(Cc,Dc)P(C,Dc)P(Cc,D)=P(C)P(D|C)P(Cc)P(D|Cc)P(C)P(Dc|C)P(Cc)P(D|Cc)=OR

(c)
Since P(C,D) also equals P(D)P(C|D), reversing the roles of C and D in part b gives the result.
