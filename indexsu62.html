2.1.19 problem 21

(a)
The sample space is (H,H,H),(H,H,T),(H,T,H),(T,H,H). Since each outcome is equally likely, the answer is 14.
(b)
Since the last throw is independent of the first two, the probability that all three throws landed heads given two of them landed heads equals the probability that the third throw landed heads, which is 12.
