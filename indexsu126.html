3.3.1 problem 38

(a)
Let Y=X+1. Then, X and Y are clearly dependent, and P(X<Y)=1.
(b)
Let X be the value of a toss of a six sided die, with values 1 to 6. Let Y be the value of a toss of a six sided die, with values 7 to 12. Tosses of the two die are independent, but P(X<Y)=1.
