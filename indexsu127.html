3.3.2 problem 39

Let X have a discrete uniform distribution over values 1,2,3,...10. Let Y=11−X. Then Y is also discrete uniform over the same sample space, but P(X=Y)=0.

If X and Y are independent, then P(X=Y)=∑i∈SP(X=i)P(Y=i)>0.
